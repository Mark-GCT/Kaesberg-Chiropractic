WHO WE ARE

PROFESSIONAL CHIROPRACTIC CARE IN BELLEVILLE & EAST ST. LOUIS

At Kaesberg Chiropractic Clinic PC, we are committed to the health and vitality of our patients. Our goal is always to treat your health issues and alleviate aches and pains naturally through chiropractic care and physical therapy. Our knowledgeable doctors and friendly staff members can help you relieve pain from accidental injuries, sports injuries and more.

Dr. Kendra Kaesberg-White and Dr. James White have owned and operated Kaesberg Chiropractic Clinic PC since 1984. They met in college, married in 1985 and have three children: Benjamin, Michelle and Bruce. They have continued the family atmosphere established in 1950 by Dr. Kendra’s father, Dr. Norman T. Kaesberg, who was a licensed chiropractor, physical therapist and acupuncturist. Additionally, Dr. Kaesberg-White and Dr. White have co-authored five published articles. Our practice has two convenient locations in Belleville and East St. Louis. Additionally, we have been associated with the American Spine Center since 2003.
