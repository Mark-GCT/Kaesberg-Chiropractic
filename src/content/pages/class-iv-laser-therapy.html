HOW DOES LASER THERAPY WORK?

Class IV laser therapy sends a stimulated light of a specific wavelength deep into the affected bone and muscle tissue 2-4 inches below the surface and signals damaged cells to produce energy, which helps speed up the healing process. The laser also causes a chemical reaction to occur in the cells, which decreases the pain in that area of the body.

This therapy has proven to be effective in stimulating damaged cells, helping to promote faster healing which can be targeted to a specific area of the body.
